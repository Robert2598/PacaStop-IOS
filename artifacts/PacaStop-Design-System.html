Motion

  • Savings counter ticks live (last digits move every second).
  • Panic countdown ring depletes over 60s; button softly pulses red.
  • Jackpot marquee bulbs blink in a staggered chase.
  • Current car gently floats.
  • House-edge simulation bars drain left→right (lime → red).
  • Sheets slide up; screens/sections fade up on entrance.
